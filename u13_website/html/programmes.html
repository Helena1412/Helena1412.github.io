Camp Canada contains different types of camps which are:

Traditional which is the most popular that focus on outdoor activities , water-sports, arts and crafts.
Underprivileged camps provide the same activities as traditional camps.
Girl Guides offer to young girls in the Girl Guide movement.Highest campers and staff will live in platform tents.
Wilderness aimed to focus on outdoors activities like canoeing,hiking and camping.
Religious camps deliver range of activities that are run by Christian or Jewish organizations,the purpose is to respect and understant other people's beliefs.
Physical Disabilitythey are concentrated to children with pysical Disabilities,activities are adapted to ensure the enjoyment of their experience.
Learning/Mental Disability and Developmental Delay Camps are attended by children,camps activities are adaptedto ensure the enjoyment of their experience and help them to develop their talents
